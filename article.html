Vermont Leads the US, But Oregon Is the Safer Bet

The US dataset is the most precise of the three. The National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) flags individual reports as "Possible Abduction" or "Missing Time", collectively called close encounter or CE reports.

Of 18,561 US sighting reports filed between 2020 and 2024, 609 carried an abduction flag. Those 609 reports spread across 49 states. South Dakota recorded zero. Louisiana recorded one.

United States: What the Data Shows

  • Vermont leads at 10.89 per million, but carries only 7 total reports. One unusual filing changes the rate materially. Oregon is the more robust benchmark.

  • New Hampshire ranks 3rd, notable as the site of the 1961 Betty and Barney Hill case, the most documented close-encounter report in US history.
  • California: 61 reports, 32nd place. The numbers do not lie. Population always wins.

How We Did This

What we measured: The likelihood that any individual resident of a given US state, Canadian city, or UK city would file a UFO or abduction-flagged report over the 2020 to 2024 period, expressed as a rate per million residents, a 1-in-X five-year odds figure, and a lifetime chance percentage over 80 years.

US data: Abduction-flagged reports, those marked "Possible Abduction" or "Missing Time" and collectively called close encounter (CE) reports, from the National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC) dataset accessed via Hugging Face (kcimc/NUFORC, nuforc_bool.csv). 609 abduction-flagged reports from 18,561 total US reports 2020-2024. Population: US Census Bureau Vintage 2025 state estimates.

Canadian data: Total UFO sighting reports from the Canadian UFO Survey annual PDFs 2020-2024 (canadianuforeport.ca), produced by Ufology Research. City counts from confirmed metro tables in the 2022 and 2024 survey essays. Population: Statistics Canada subprovincial estimates July 2025, city proper figures. Halifax flagged in the 2024 survey as potentially overrepresented due to active Nova Scotia UFO social media networks.

UK data: Total UFO sighting reports from UFO Identified annual reports 2020-2024 (ufoidentified.co.uk), collected via 50+ Freedom of Information (FOI) requests to UK police forces plus social media monitoring. City counts from the confirmed 2020-2023 cumulative interactive map. 2024 totals estimated at +25% uplift. Population: Office for National Statistics (ONS) mid-2023 estimates, October 2024.

Cross-country note: The US uses abduction-flagged reports only. Canada and the UK use total sighting reports. Direct numerical comparison across countries is not valid. Data extracted and calculations finalised June 2026.
